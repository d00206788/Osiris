About

The band comprises singer Jack Conway, Dylan Tuohy and Ciaran Leyden on guitars, Kyle Duncan on bass and Jamie Myers on drums. Having got together early last year, they began writing their guitar-driven own songs, making a big impression when they took part in the Louth heat of the Irish Youth Music Awards 2022.
